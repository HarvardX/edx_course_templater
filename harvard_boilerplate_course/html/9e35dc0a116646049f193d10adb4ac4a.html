Final Notes

This covers the majority of the problems you will see in this course. Keep these final notes in mind as you work out the answers to our questions:

  1. Be sure to check your formatting when you enter numerical responses. Don't get frustrated; computers can be picky.
  2. Sometimes our problems are randomized so that each student sees a slightly different problem. You may see different numbers, text, or even images, from what other students see. You may see problems in different orders, or in different locations.
  3. When asking for help on the discussion forum, don't say things like "I answered D and it was wrong! Why?" because that gives away an answer. You shouldn't give away even wrong answers.
  4. Don't be afraid to use the (insert link to discussion forum). We are all here to support one another!
