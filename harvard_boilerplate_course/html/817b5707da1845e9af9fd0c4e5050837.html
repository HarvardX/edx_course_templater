Pop-Up Questions

You can duplicate the top component on this page and place it above any video to make it a pop-up video. You will need to edit that component to adjust the <script> tag. When you're done the "title" entries should match the titles of your problems, and the "time" entries should match when you want them to show up. Hx-js will do the rest!

Notes:

  • As with all javascript, formatting is important. The last item in the timer list does not end with a comma, but the rest of them do. You also need to match the exact title of your problem.
  • You can enter the time in seconds or mm:ss format. Decimals are ok.
  • It is recommended you don't use this more than every 30 seconds, preferably longer. You should also ask short questions.
  • Remember that your learners can always turn off or skip the problems, and they'll still be able to answer them later because they're farther down on the page.
